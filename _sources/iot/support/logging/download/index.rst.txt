=================
Download IoT Logs
=================

There is different ways to download the IoT logs depending on the environment you are using.
If one method fail, you can try the other one. They will be ordered by convenience.

.. note::
   .. versionadded:: core-march-2024

   .. https://github.com/odoo/odoo/pull/150920

   The Iot logs are automatically sent to the Odoo's server logs.
   If you are on odoo.sh you can browse the IoT logs directly and have the 
   full history
